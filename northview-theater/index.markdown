## Learning Resources

New to sound? Start here:

1. Watch the [YouTube Playlist](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLA24qkj70n741DsXFHOuKCFP2eKmifpwR&si=D8dAgczXjsKoJiuW) - especially "Adam Savage Explores the Sound Mixing of Hamilton!"
2. Read through your [role guide](#find-your-role) above
3. Practice mic placement on a friend before tech week
4. Ask questions - the sound lead is here to help!

**Remember:** The audience doesn't know what's supposed to happen. If you handle problems professionally, they'll never know anything went wrong!
