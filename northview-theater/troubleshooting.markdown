# Sound System Troubleshooting Guide

[Back to Sound Crew Hub](/northview-theater/)

---

Even with great prep, things will go wrong - a mic cuts out, a music cue won't play, or the audience hears feedback. This guide helps you diagnose and fix common issues quickly, **without panic**, whether you're at the board or backstage.

---

## Quick Troubleshooting Flow

When something isn't working:

**STOP -> CHECK -> FIX -> RESET**

1. **Stay calm**
2. **Lower the fader and mute the channel** if needed
3. **Check connections, batteries, and settings**
4. **Communicate quietly and clearly** with other crew
5. **Fix the issue or swap gear**
6. **Fade in again smoothly** when ready

---

## MIC & ACTOR PROBLEMS

| Problem | What to Check & Do |
|---------|-------------------|
| **No sound from a mic** | - Is the pack turned on?<br>- Is the mic muted on the board?<br>- Is the fader down?<br>- Is the receiver showing signal? |
| **Mic cuts in and out** | - Weak battery?<br>- Cable connection loose?<br>- Antennas blocked (actor facing backstage wall)?<br>- RF interference? |
| **Mic sounds muffled** | - Mic is under costume or hair?<br>- Windshield missing?<br>- EQ needs high-mid boost? |
| **Mic sounds harsh or tinny** | - Too much EQ in 3-6 kHz range?<br>- Mic too close to mouth (cheek placement)? |
| **Feedback when fader goes up** | - Is the actor in front of a speaker?<br>- Try panning slightly away<br>- Lower gain<br>- Use narrow EQ cut in feedback zone |

> If unsure: swap actor into a pre-labeled **backup pack + headset**.

---

## BATTERY & PACK ISSUES

| Problem | What to Check & Do |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Mic dies mid-scene** | - Hand actor a **backup pack** (faster than swapping batteries) |
| **Battery runs out unexpectedly** | - Was it replaced before the show?<br>- Was it an old/reused battery? |
| **Receiver shows "No Link"** | - Mic pack may have been turned off<br>- Re-link to receiver if needed |

---

## MIXER & BOARD ISSUES

| Problem | What to Check & Do |
|---------|-------------------|
| **No sound from board at all** | - Is the **Main L/R muted**?<br>- Are speakers powered on?<br>- Is the scene correct? |
| **Mic sounds distorted** | - Input gain too high<br>- Receiver gain too high<br>- Mic placed too close |
| **Sudden volume jump/drop** | - Fader bumped?<br>- Scene reloaded?<br>- Check for automation or effects |
| **Solo light is on** | - Someone hit "Solo" - un-solo to return sound to Main Mix |

---

## MUSIC CUE ISSUES

| Problem | What to Check & Do |
|---------|-------------------|
| **No music is playing** | - Laptop volume up?<br>- Cable connected to correct input?<br>- Playback software paused? |
| **Music playing but not heard** | - Fader down?<br>- Aux In muted?<br>- Music not routed to Main L/R? |
| **Music too quiet/loud** | - Adjust fader, not laptop volume (unless laptop is maxed) |
| **Distortion or hum** | - 3.5mm cable going bad?<br>- Ground loop (use DI box)?<br>- Try unplugging power brick from laptop |

---

## BACKUP PLAN TIPS

- Keep **1-2 backup mic packs**, **cables**, and **taped headsets** ready to go
- Keep a **backup music source** (phone or USB stick) with key cues loaded
- Have a **small toolkit** with:
  - Tape
  - Batteries
  - Cable testers
  - Label maker or Sharpie
  - Windscreens

---

## For New Crew Members

- It's okay to not know everything - your job is to notice issues and communicate
- Always **start with the simple stuff**: is it turned on? Is it muted? Is the battery okay?
- If something's broken, **don't hide it** - report it and help solve it
